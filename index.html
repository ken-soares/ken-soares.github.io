About me

Hello, I am Kenneth Soares, a highschool student, hobbyist developer and a bit of a weeb.

I started to code few years ago because I wanted to make video games and websites. Nowadays, I do not specialize in any form of development as I like to learn a bit about everything there is to know. I am currently learning about rust and javascript as well as linux which is my OS of choice. I really like the philosophy behind open-source software which is to me like a world where everyone knows, or is able to know, what he is using everyday and how to contribute to it. I also play video games, especially rhythm games such as osu! and I also like to watch some anime from time to time.

Currently in my second to last year of highschool, I am planning on graduating with computer science and math as my main subjects next year and after that, I'll try to enter an engeneering school or a coding licence.
